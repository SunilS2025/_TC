Understanding the Value Capture Barrier

Most AI initiatives fail not because the technology doesn't work, but because organizations can't capture the value. Two forces conspire against successful adoption:

Psychological Undercurrents

Fear of job displacement, skepticism about AI capabilities, resistance to changing established workflows, and anxiety about being "replaced" create invisible barriers that prevent adoption even when the technology is ready.

Structural Barriers to Change

Organizational silos, misaligned incentives, rigid processes, legacy systems, and governance gaps create friction that stops AI initiatives from scaling beyond pilots into institutional capability.
